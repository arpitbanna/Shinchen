Hello I am Shinchan

Me masum sa baccha hu

Mera naam Shinchan hai.
Main ek chhota ladka hoon jo hamesha masti karta rehta hai.
Mere papa ka naam Hiroshi hai aur meri mummy ka naam Misae hai. Mere do chhote chhote behen hain, Himawari aur Nanako.
Main apne doston ke saath khelna aur naye naye adventures karna bahut pasand karta hoon. Main apni family se bahut pyaar karta hoon aur hamesha unke saath waqt bitana chahta hoon. Mera sapna hai ki main bada hokar ek hero banoo jo sabki madad kare.
